About

I'm a veteran LSRP player going by the name of James Woka. I've been actively playing LSRP and making YouTube content about LSRP for roughly 3 years. During these years, I've been a part of both SASD and LSPD and achieved the ranks of Staff Sergeant & Sergeant in Training, respectively. I was a part of many units as well as unit command in certain ones.

Recently I got banned from LSRP for trying to sell my IG cash for RL money. With years of experience in SASD and LSPD, I plan to offer my services and help people attempting to get into these factions. Due to my high ranks I had authority to mark and check various applications submitted by people, hence writing them myself is not going to be challenging for me.

Why Buy Applications?

LSRP is a huge SAMP community. The standards for the community are high. Therefore it is often very difficult for the average person to apply for weapon licenses, official factions and even progress within these official factions. Their applications require lengthy essays which require effort. Some people are lazy or just not capable of doing these which is why I offer to do them for you.

If you're too busy or you constantly fail to achieve the quality standard the LSRP community wants in your essay questions then this is the service you need. After years of experience in LSRP, I am very well at writing the essay questions and can provide genuine content within 24-48 hours.
